Changelog for 2.3.5 "Jung"

Fixes:
- Fixed an issue with Medical Treatment being possible without Physician.
- You can no longer conduct medical expeditions to your own country, as that is too close.
- You can no longer conduct medical expeditions if you don't have the correct technology or if you already found a treatment.
- The yellow fever treatment now works correctly.

Map:
- Diseases no longer use the same map texture as droughts.
