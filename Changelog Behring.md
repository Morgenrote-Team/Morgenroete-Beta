Changelog for 2.3.4 "Behring"

Medical Expansion:
- Added one possible event for each physician specialization.
- Added events for Lombrosism.

Epidemics:
- Health Care Level 1 is needed (instead of 2) to prevent smallpox.
- It is possible to eredicate smallpox. To achieve this, every country needs the smallpox vaccine and at least Health Care Level 1.

Economy:
- Added rubber as a potential luxury good for rich POPs.
- Synthetic Rubber output increased from 60 -> 90.
- Synthetic Oil output increased from 60 -> 90.

Achievements:
- Zauberberg: Own a tuberculosis sanatorium and a writer.
- Who needs Dunant?: Found the Red Cross without playing Switzerland.
- Trick or Treatment: Obtain a treatment for all ten epidemic diseases.
- John Slow: Be the first nation to find a treatment against cholera.
- Eradication of the old Foe: Pursue the global eradication of smallpox.
- Minderwertigkeitskomplex: Establish your own Psychological School
- Face of a Criminal: Eliminate a character because of his physiognomy.

Characters:
- Added DNA for a few characters.

Fixes:
- Fixed an issue with Medical Techniques not removing old modifiers and triggering the false event.
- Fixed notification not showing the name of the Doctor when accelerating research.
- Countries formed by the USA can now build the Statue of Liberty as well
- Character Traits in the sports tab should now also show tooltips.
